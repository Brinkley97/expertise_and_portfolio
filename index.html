Bio

A Kingdom Man seeking to fulfill my Kingdom purpose - Share the Gospel of JESUS. From a rural city in Georgia called Thomson in McDuffie County.

I finished my MS in Computer Science at the University of Southern California (USC) December 2024. I'm in search of a Ph.D. advisor in time series for August 2024. Skip to Research section for more. I research and write code using conventional time series (TS) methods along with more modern machine learning (ML) techniques to make create forecasts. While technology's influence on (1) personalized health and (2) personalized finances plague my mind often, I'm open to analyzing and building models in other domains. I'm also looking for paid gigs (ie: forecast analyst, quantitative finance, ML, research scientist, software engineer) between January 2024 - August 2024.

I'm also Co-Founder of Catalyst Robotics, where we are building autonomous mobile health clinics that bring self-service healthcare screenings directly to patients. At Catalyst, I primarily create systems and develop software for two projects - (1) autonomous perception and navigation and (2) non-invasive face scan to check pulse, body temperature, and other health metrics.

Have you noticed the "Fit To..." above? In 2018, I started my FitToCode brand in undergrad at Claflin University. Since August 2021, I paused developing my brand to focus on graduate school. We'll be back soon, so stay tuned!
